Pair Programming

Pair programming is the practice of two people collaborating on the same coding challenge. One these challenges, a person is designated navigator and the other the driver.

The Navigator

The navigator is typically the director of ideas, guiding the direction of the content. The navigator communicates directives to the driver.

The Driver

In pair programming, the driver typically is the person at the keyboard. They take instruction from and implement the ideas of the navigator.

  • Enters code
  • Takes direction from the navigator
